Who We Are

GEOMAG TERRATECH LIMITED is an innovation-driven construction engineering company with expertise in providing civil engineering services, procurement, surveying and mapping, and drone services for site development, public works, and urban infrastructure projects.

Incorporated in Nigeria under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020 on November 9, 2017, Geomag Terratech boasts excellent service delivery achieved through a combination of years of experience, up-to-date equipment, and leveraging emerging technologies. We ensure consistent, accurate, and timely delivery of service.

Our team includes registered professional engineers, surveyors, expert field personnel, and office staff who work tirelessly to ensure our clients are satisfied. We are experienced in public and private site work and institutional projects of various sizes and complexity.
