Who We Are

GEOMAG TERRATECH LIMITED is an innovation-driven construction engineering company with expertise in civil engineering, procurement, surveying & mapping, and drone services for site development, public works and urban infrastructure. Incorporated in Nigeria under the Companies & Allied Matters Act in 2017, Geomag combines years of practical experience with modern equipment and emerging technology to deliver accurate, timely and cost-effective project outcomes.

Core Capabilities

  • Civil engineering & site development
  • Surveying & mapping (RTK-capable drones and high precision equipment)
  • Drone-enabled aerial surveying & inspections (Matrice 350 RTK, Phantom 4 RTK)
  • Procurement & project logistics

Why Clients Choose Us

Reliable delivery, professional team (registered engineers & surveyors), up-to-date equipment and a commitment to sustainable and affordable engineering solutions.
